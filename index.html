    • Kingdom Seekers Fellowship

    Kingdom Seekers Fellowship is an Apostolic Church founded on true Biblical Teachings and Christian Principles, Holy Spirit empowered and driven. The church theme has been drawn from Christ’s teaching as amplified in Matthew 6:33 ‘ BUT SEEK YE FIRST THE KINGDOM OF GOD AND HIS RIGHTEOUSNESS AND ALL OTHER THINGS SHALL BE ADDED UNTO YOU’. Kingdom Seekers Fellowship was founded 12 years ago by Apostle John Kimani William together with His Wife Rev. Naomi Kimani who since then have continued to minister the word of God to an ever growing congregation . The Ministry is Headquartered in Nakuru Town with over 20 Branches Spreading across the country. The main Mission of Kingdom Seekers Fellowship is to transform Lives by seeking God and making Him Known.

    Vision

    God Seekers who fulfill the Purposes of God in their Generation in Kenya and beyond The Vision of the ministry is not in planting more churches but intentionally putting all efforts in strengthening the Missionary arm of the Ministry dubbed Missions to the Body of Christ International MBCI.

    Mission

    To transform lives by seeking God and making Him known Kingdom Seekers Fellowship is a church founded on true Biblical teachings and Christian principles, Holy Spirit empowered and driven. The Church’s theme has been drawn from Christ’s teaching as amplified in Matthew 6:33: “But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.”

    Values

    The core values of the Church are; • Humility – Having a servant-hood spirit. • Integrity – Honest, transparent, dependable, consistent person. • Holy Living – Keeping to the biblical moral standards. • Prayer – Seeking God’s guidance in everything, declaring dependence on God. • Excellence – Consistently high standards of doing things in all areas. • Stewardship – accountable in your area of service.
